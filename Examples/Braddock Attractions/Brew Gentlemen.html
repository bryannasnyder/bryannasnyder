Brew Gentlemen

Started by students from Carnegie Mellon University, Brew Gentlemen provides delicious craft beer and local cider, and a great atmosphere. They began their journey in an old supply store and have expanded to encompass the adjoining building and the property behind theirs (deemed BG Open Air) which is a gorgeous outdoor space. BG is expanding in Braddock and betting on it’s revitalization!

Located on Braddock Avenue, it is hard to miss. As a regular, try the Mexican Coffee.
