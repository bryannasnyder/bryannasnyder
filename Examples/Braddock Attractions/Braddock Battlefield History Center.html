Braddock Battlefield History Center

Recently updated, Braddock’s Battlefield History Center is a wonderful place to learn about Braddock’s history. They have real artifacts, replica uniforms, and detailed information about British Major General Edward Braddock’s defeat at the Battle of the Monongahela.

Visits are only $5!
