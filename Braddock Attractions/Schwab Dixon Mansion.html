Schwab-Dixon Mansion

The Richardsonian Romanesque mansion was built in 1888 for a steel mill employee, Charles Schwab. Steel Mill employees cycled through until being purchased by the school district. Dr. Bruce Dixon purchased it in 1983 hoping to restore it to it’s former glory, but was unable to complete the repairs. In 2014, the mansion was purchased by a retired Pitt Professor, Dr. John Hemple to pick up where Dr. Dixon left off and is currently not available for tours. Schwixon can be found just up the road from the Braddock Library.
